About me

I enjoy taking ideas from start to finish while getting my hands dirty in the process, which usually involves working in the machine shop to bring my designs to life using the mill and lathe. I plan to use my experience with programming and building Arduino projects as a stepping stone to learn about PLCs and their implementation in manufacturing facilities.

I learned that for a team to thrive, every member has to contribute to reach a common goal. It was my job as captain to motivate my teammates to continuously improve and try their best. I learned to communicate effictively with other players and coaches to talk strategy and reflect on past plays, and I developed strong interpersonal skills that are transferrable to the workplace.
