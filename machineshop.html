Machine Shop Designs

The drawings below showcase some of the parts I created using the mill, lathe, and other shop tools over the course of my previous co-op. Each of these parts was used for a particular project in the manufacturing facility. While machining these parts I learned about speeds and feeds by reading the machinist's handbook and then applied those principles and formulas while machining to manufacture higher quality parts. On my co-op I wasn't required to use the machine shop for any purposes but I took initiative to ask the machinist on site to teach me because of my strong desire to learn. I had no previous machining experience before this.

Saw Bracket

This is the first part that I designed, created and implemented with the use of a mill, bandsaw and sander. The part is an attachment for a handsaw that is used to cut off brickmoulds from windows in the mulling department. I created the design for it after studying the profile of the brickmould and determining what needed to be cut off.

Mounting Legs

I designed these mounting legs to be compatible with 80/20 aluminum extrusions. I used the 80/20 aluminum extrusions to create sign holders for the 5S initiative, and created these legs as a method to fasten the sign to the end of a table.

Proximity Sensor Parts

I designed this part for a maintenance worker who wanted to change out these mechanical devices located on the weld machines for proximity sensors. I designed this part to fit inside a counterbore located on a handle. The maintenance worker would be able to screw in the proximity sensor to a desired distance. The parts have very tight tolereances due to the small size of them.

Mounting Blocks

I designed these mounting plates to screw onto the sides of a cardboard cutter that would then be mounted on and MDF table top. I took measurments of the max depth that the cardboard cutter could cut and of the thickness of cardboard that would need to slide underneath to determine the thickness of these plates. I made a total of 4 identical plates using the mill and horizontal bandsaw.
